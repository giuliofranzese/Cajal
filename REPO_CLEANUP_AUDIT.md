## Recommended next cleanup opportunities (manual decision)

These are likely intentional research/demo artifacts, so they were **not** removed automatically:

1. **Version large datasets/checkpoints outside Git**
   - Consider moving `functional_kl/code/data/**` and `functional_kl/code/checkpoints/**` to remote storage and documenting download scripts.
2. **Avoid committing generated slide assets where reproducible**
   - Folders like `*/lecture_files/` and `*/slides/*_files/` are generated by Quarto/reveal.js and may be reproducible from source `.qmd`.
3. **Consolidate duplicated assets**
   - Example: `soi/assets/**` and `soi/slides/assets/**` appear to overlap.
4. **Separate source vs deliverables**
   - Keep source (`.qmd`, notebooks, code) under version control, and move heavy exports (`.pdf`, large `.zip`) to release artifacts.

## Suggested policy

- Add a lightweight CI check that warns on:
  - macOS artifacts (`.DS_Store`, `__MACOSX`, `._*`)
  - editor directories (`.vscode/`)
  - files over a size threshold (e.g., 10 MB) unless explicitly allowlisted
